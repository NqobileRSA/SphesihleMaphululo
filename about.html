Our Creche

Founded in 2011, our daycare in Umlazi is dedicated to providing a nurturing and enriching environment for children. We prioritize holistic development, emphasizing academic readiness, social-emotional growth, physical well-being, and creativity.

Our curriculum is designed to spark curiosity and foster a love for learning through play-based activities and hands-on experiences. At the heart of our daycare are our dedicated educators, who are passionate about early childhood education and committed to supporting each child's unique talents and interests.

We believe in the importance of collaboration with families, caregivers, and the wider community to create a supportive network that uplifts and empowers every child. Located in the vibrant community of Umlazi, we celebrate diversity and embrace the cultural richness of our surroundings.

Together, we strive to shape a brighter future for the children of Umlazi, providing them with the tools and opportunities they need to thrive and succeed.

Rules and Regulations

ARRIVAL 06:00-08:30

Parents are requested to accompany their children into the school building on arrival and on departure. A Time Sheet will be provided at the door for parents to sign.

BACK GATE

This gate is locked for your child's safety and will only be used by staff members.

DEPARTURE TIME

The front door opens strictly at 12:30. All transport arrangements such as friends, family members etc, are parents responsibilities as the school does not involve itself in any external matter. The school must be notified about any changes in transport arrangements. Children become distressed when they are being fetched late, therefore will be taken to aftercare at 16h00 when parents are late and parents will be charged the aftercare fees. The school does provide its own transport which transports LATTER GLORY DAY CARE pupils ONLY to and from school.

ILLNESS

No child who is ill may be sent to school. No medication may be administered by staff, if such treatment is required. Please keep your child at home if he/she is sick. Infectious disease must be reported and a doctor's certificate/note is requested for clearance for disease like Conjunctivitis (Pink Eye) in order for the school to allow him/her to come back to school.

FOOD

Breakfast is provided at school every day. Parents provide snacks like Fruits, Yoghurt, Healthy biscuits etc for Snack time at 10h00 and for aftercare snack at 16h15. Fruit must be prepared at home, staff members will NOT peel fruits for anyone. Pupils must bring cooked meals, sandwich, hot dogs etc for lunch at 12h30 with 100% Juice/Milk and Water. Sweets are NOT allowed at school. Chips are only allowed on FRIDAYS.

SCHOOL HOURS

From 06:00-16:00

AFTER CARE

From 16:00-17:30

PARENTS DUTIES

Please ensure that:

  • All clothing and shoes are clearly marked.
  • Children are dressed in proper LATTER GLORY DAY CARE uniform Monday – Thursday. Friday they can wear anything.
  • Please include one extra sets of clothing in your child's school bag in case of small accidents.
  • Children must wear white footwear with their uniforms, which includes sandals or shoes and not thongs, clogs or high heels. They may also come barefoot. Please ensure that your child wears comfortable shoes on for outings.

PARENT INTEREST AND PARTICIPATION

  • We welcome parent's interest as we see our role as teachers as an extension of home and team effort with parent.
  • Appointment and interviews with staff can be arranged through consultation with the principal.
  • Social functions, as well as fundraising events are arranged from time to time.

BIRTHDAY RINGS

These are celebrated at school and we ask that you organize with your child's teacher when your child's birthday is nearly due. Each child will celebrate his/her birthday with his/her own class. Parents may attend their child's birthday ring. We will not allow birthday parties with gifts and invitations.

NO TOYS, sweets, books, jewellery, medicine, cell phones, etc. May be brought to school.

TRANSPORT

We do offer transportation to and from school. For surrounding areas at a monthly payment fee.

Thank you for your cooperation.
