Welcome to Latter Glory Day Care

A cherished institution that has been nurturing young minds since its inception in 2011. Situated in the heart of Umlazi, our daycare is more than just a place for children to spend their days; it's a warm, welcoming environment where little ones embark on their journey of discovery, learning, and growth. With a dedicated team of caregivers and educators, we provide a safe and stimulating space where children can explore, play, and develop essential skills for their future. Our commitment to excellence and unwavering passion for early childhood education have made us a trusted choice for families in the community. At Latter Glory, every child is valued, celebrated, and supported as they take their first steps towards a bright and promising future.
